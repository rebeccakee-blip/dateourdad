The Short Version

Sharp dresser. Sharper wit. Makes the best pancakes.

Richard is a retired architect who spent his career designing projects around San Francisco – and in his retirement he's designed a life that's somehow even busier. He sings in a chorus, bowls on a team, plays bocce, travels internationally, reads extensively, and still finds time to read a meme aloud to you whether you asked or not. Also, he can dance.

His house is immaculate. His opinions are well-formed. His sense of whimsy is fully intact, and it runs right alongside a genuinely strong intellect – which, in our experience, is a rarer combination than people think.
